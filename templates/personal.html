Personal Projects

Training Room
A Django application for certification exam preparation.

Training Room is a platform that allows users to prepare for certification exams through practical questions. Key features include:

  • User registration and personalized dashboard.
  • Addition of questions with detailed information, including related area and certification.
  • View questions created by other users.
  • Identification of the question creator for contact.

This application is designed to help companies create a customized preparation environment with a clean and effective question database.

The Training Room project provides a comprehensive platform for exam preparation and question-solving. Users can register, access a dashboard with questions related to various certification exams, and create their questions for others to solve. Each question can include additional information, such as the relevant area and certification category, facilitating easy filtering.

Furthermore, Training Room includes an API that allows other companies in the same industry to access and utilize the database of solved questions. This collaborative feature enables access to questions created by partner companies, fostering a community of knowledge sharing and exam preparation.

For a live demonstration, you can visit the Training Room Demo.
